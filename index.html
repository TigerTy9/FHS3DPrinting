About Our Club

At FHS3DPrinting Club, students can explore cutting-edge technology, work on exciting projects, and learn valuable skills. We focus on structured learning and creative freedom, allowing members to grow their expertise in 3D printing.

Club Structure & Meeting Schedule

Kickoff Meeting: Introduction & Inspiration

We’ll introduce 3D printing with a showcase of exciting projects, followed by an icebreaker activity where you’ll design a simple 3D object like a keychain using Tinkercad.

Weekly/Monthly Themes: Structured Learning

Each week/month, we’ll have skill-building workshops on 3D modeling software, 3D printer troubleshooting, and virtual tours or guest speakers to explore 3D printing in the real world.

Project-Based Learning: Collaborative & Fun

Work on exciting group projects or participate in design challenges. We’ll also hold an end-of-semester showcase where members can present their projects to the school.

Open Lab Sessions: Creative Freedom

Members can come in for free design time, working on personal projects or collaborating with peers. Experienced members will mentor newcomers in these open lab sessions.

Fun & Social Elements

Engage in 3D printing games, challenges, and even field trips to local makerspaces and companies that use 3D printing technology.

Final Project & Reflection

Our club will finish with a major group project, such as a 3D-printed mural or sculpture for the school. Members will reflect on their learning and help improve the club for next year.
